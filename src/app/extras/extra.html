When one forgetfully misses a wajib act, they continue salah as usual. At the final sitting, they will recite the Tashahhud, and say salam looking over to the right shoulder immediately after Tashahhud and Shahadah, then go back into sajdah. They will perform 2 sajdahs and sit back up in the position of qa'dah. Now, they will recite Tashahhud again and complete the final qa'dah in the same manner one would finish any other salah.

Although it is haram to purposefully miss the salah in its specified time, if someone misses the time of prayer then he must make up the prayer. This is known as performing qada' salah. In performing qada' salah, it is only required to perform the Fard and Wajib Sets of the prayer.
Ex: If Zaid missed Zuhr Prayer, he only has to make up the 4 Fard that's in the Zuhr Prayer
