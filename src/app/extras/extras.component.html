When one forgetfully misses a wajib act, they continue salah as usual. At the final sitting, they will recite the Tashahhud, and say salam looking over to the right shoulder immediately after Tashahhud and Shahadah, then go back into sajdah. They will perform 2 sajdahs and sit back up in the position of qa'dah. Now, they will recite Tashahhud again and complete the final qa'dah in the same manner one would finish any other salah.

Although it is haram to purposefully miss the salah in its specified time, if someone misses the time of prayer then he must make up the prayer. This is known as performing qada' salah. In performing qada' salah, it is only required to perform the Fard and Wajib Sets of the prayer.
Ex: If Zaid missed Zuhr Prayer, he only has to make up the 4 Fard that's in the Zuhr Prayer

When a Muslim leaves this world, then it is mandatory upon the Muslim community of the deceased to properly prepare his or her Janazah. This includes the ghusl (bathing) of the deceased, performing the funeral prayer for the deceased and burying them properly.

The Salah al-Janazah is performed by men only and must be performed outside of the Masjid. The deceased should be laid in front of the Imam and the rest of the people performing the prayer will be lined up in rows behind the Imam, similar to how they are in normal congregation. Then, everyone must make the intention of performing the funeral prayer. The Imam will say "Allahu Akbar" raising his hands and tying them under his navel just how one stands in qiyam for the usual salah. The followers will do the same. At this same time, everyone should quietly recite the thana with just one small addition.
